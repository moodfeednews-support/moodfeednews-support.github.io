Most news apps serve the same relentless stream, no matter how you're feeling. MoodFeedNews is different. It organises the day's stories by mood and category, so you decide the tone of your feed — uplifting, calm, in-depth, or straight-to-the-facts.

Behind the scenes, MoodFeedNews aggregates a hand-curated, geographically balanced network of news sources spanning North America, the UK and Europe, the Middle East, Africa, South and East Asia, Oceania, and Latin America. Pick your country, pick your language, and add exactly the outlets you trust — from global names to regional voices that other apps ignore.

It's fast, private, and entirely in your control: no account required, refresh on your own schedule, and a clean reading experience with no clutter. Tap a story to read it your way — a quick in-app preview, or straight to the original page.

Built for the world. A fully localized interface in dozens of languages and a source library that reaches far beyond the English-speaking web.

Feature highlights

🎭 Mood-based feed — headlines grouped by tone and category, not just chronology

🌍 Global source network — curated outlets across every continent, organised by country

🗣️ Massively multilingual — interface and sources across dozens of languages

🔎 Your sources, your way — a built-in picker to enable exactly the outlets you want

⚡ Open links directly — jump straight to the original article, or use the in-app preview

⏱️ Refresh on your schedule — you control how often news updates

🔒 Private by default — no sign-up, no noise, just the news
